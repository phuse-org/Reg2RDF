" 872.4200 
dental handpiece and accessories. 
(a) identification. a dental handpiece and accessories is an ac-powered, water-powered, air-powered, or belt-driven, hand-held device that may include a foot controller for regulation of speed and direction of rotation or a contra-angle attachment for difficult to reach areas intended to prepare dental cavities for restorations, such as fillings, and for cleaning teeth. 
(b) classification. class i.
