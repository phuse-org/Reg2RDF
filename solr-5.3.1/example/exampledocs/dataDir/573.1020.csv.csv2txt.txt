yellow prussiate of soda. 
yellow prussiate of soda (sodium ferrocyanide decahydrate: na fe(cn) -10h o) may be safely used as an anticaking agent in salt for animal consumption at a level not to exceed 13 parts per million. the additive contains a minimum of 99.0 percent by weight of sodium ferrocyanide decahydrate.
