medical image hardcopy device. 
(a) identification. a medical image hardcopy device is a device that produces a visible printed record of a medical image and associated identification information. examples include multiformat cameras and laser printers. 
(b) classification. class ii (special controls; voluntary standardsdigital imaging and communications in medicine (dicom) std., joint photographic experts group (jpeg) std., society of motion picture and television engineers (smpte) test pattern).
