" 1315.27 
abandonment of quota. 
any manufacturer assigned an individual manufacturing quota for a chemical pursuant to  1315.23 may at any time abandon his right to manufacture all or any part of the quota by filing with the drug & chemical evaluation section a written notice of the abandonment, stating the name and dea chemical code number, as set forth in part 1310 of this chapter, of the chemical and the amount which he has chosen not to manufacture. the administrator may, in his discretion, allocate the amount among the other manufacturers in proportion to their respective quotas.",
