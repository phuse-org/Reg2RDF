" 872.1905 
dental x-ray film holder. 
(a) identification. a dental x-ray film holder is a device intended to position and to hold x-ray film inside the mouth. 
(b) classification. class i (general controls). the device is exempt from the premarket notification procedures in subpart e of part 807 of this chapter subject to the limitations in  872.9. if the device is not labeled or otherwise represented as sterile, it is also exempt from the current good manufacturing practice requirements of the quality system regulation in part 820 of this chapter, with the exceptions of  820.180, with respect to general requirements concerning records, and  820.198, with respect to complaint files.
