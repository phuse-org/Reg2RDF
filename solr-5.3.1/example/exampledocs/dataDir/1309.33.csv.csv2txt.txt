filing of application; joint filings. 
(a) all applications for registration shall be submitted for filing to the registration section, drug enforcement administration. see the table of dea mailing addresses in  1321.01 of this chapter for the current mailing address. the appropriate registration fee and any required attachments must accompany the application. 
(b) any person required to obtain more than one registration may submit all applications in one package. each application must be complete and must not refer to any accompanying application for required information.
