" 74.2707a 
ext. d&c yellow no. 7. 
(a) identity and specifications. the color additive ext. d&c yellow no. 7 shall conform in identity and specifications to the requirements of  74.1707a (a)(1) and (b). 
(b) uses and restrictions. ext. d&c yellow no. 7 may be safely used for coloring externally applied cosmetics in amounts consistent with good manufacturing practice. 
(c) labeling. the label of the color additive shall conform to the requirements of  70.25 of this chapter. 
(d) certification. all batches of ext. d&c yellow no. 7 shall be certified in accordance with regulations in part 80 of this chapter.",
