" 872.4120 
bone cutting instrument and accessories. 
(a) identification. a bone cutting instrument and accessories is a metal device intended for use in reconstructive oral surgery to drill or cut into the upper or lower jaw and may be used to prepare bone to insert a wire, pin, or screw. the device includes the manual bone drill and wire driver, powered bone drill, rotary bone cutting handpiece, and ac-powered bone saw. 
(b) classification. class ii.",
