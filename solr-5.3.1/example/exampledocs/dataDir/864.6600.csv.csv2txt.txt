osmotic fragility test. 
(a) identification. an osmotic fragility test is a device used to determine the resistance of red blood cells to hemolysis (destruction) in varying concentrations of hypotonic saline solutions. 
(b) classification. class i (general controls). this device is exempt from the premarket notification procedures in subpart e of part 807 of this chapter subject to the limitations in  864.9.
