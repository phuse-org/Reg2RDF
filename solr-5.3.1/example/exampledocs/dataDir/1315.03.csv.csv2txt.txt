" 1315.03 
personal use exemption. 
a person need not register as an importer, file an import declaration, and obtain an import quota if both of the following conditions are met: 
(a) the person purchases scheduled listed chemical products at retail and imports them for personal use, by means of shipping through any private or commercial carrier or the postal service.
