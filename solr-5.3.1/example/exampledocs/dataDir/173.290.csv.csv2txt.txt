trichloroethylene. 
tolerances are established for residues of trichloroethylene resulting from its use as a solvent in the manufacture of foods as follows: 
decaffeinated ground coffee 25 parts per million. decaffeinated soluble (instant) coffee extract 10 parts per million. spice oleoresins 30 parts per million (provided that if residues of other chlorinated solvents are also present, the total of all residues of such solvents in spice oleoresins shall not exceed 30 parts per million).",
