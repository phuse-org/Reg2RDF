" 660.20 
blood grouping reagent. 
(a) proper name and definition. the proper name of this product shall be blood grouping reagent and it shall consist of an antibody-containing fluid containing one or more of the blood grouping antibodies listed in  660.28(d). 
(b) source. the source of this product shall be blood, plasma, serum, or protein-rich fluids, such as those derived from stable immunoglobulin-secreting cell lines maintained either in tissue cultures or in secondary hosts.
