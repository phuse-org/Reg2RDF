" 82.1307 
d&c red no. 7. 
(a) the color additive d&c red no. 7 shall conform in identity and specifications to the requirements of  74.1307 (a)(1) and (b) of this chapter. 
(b) the color additive d&c red no. 7 may be safely used for coloring drugs such that the combined total of d&c red no. 6 and d&c red no. 7 does not exceed 5 milligrams per daily dose of the drug.
