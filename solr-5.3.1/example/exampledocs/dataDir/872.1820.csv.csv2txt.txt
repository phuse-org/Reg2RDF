dental x-ray exposure alignment device. 
(a) identification. a dental x-ray exposure alignment device is a device intended to position x-ray film and to align the examination site with the x-ray beam. 
(b) classification. class i (general controls). the device is exempt from the premarket notification procedures in subpart e of part 807 of this chapter subject to the limitations in  872.9.
