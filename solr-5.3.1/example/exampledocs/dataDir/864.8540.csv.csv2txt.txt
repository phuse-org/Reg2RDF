" 864.8540 
red cell lysing reagent. 
(a) identification. a red cell lysing reagent is a device used to lyse (destroy) red blood cells for hemoglobin determinations or aid in the counting of white blood cells. 
(b) classification. class i (general controls). this device is exempt from the premarket notification procedures in subpart e of part 807 of this chapter subject to the limitations in  864.9.
