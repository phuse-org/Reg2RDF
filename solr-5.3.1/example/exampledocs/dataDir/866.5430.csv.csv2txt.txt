beta -2-glycoprotein i immunological test system. 
(a) identification. beta -2-glycoprotein i immunological test system is a device that consists of the reagents used to measure by immunochemical techniques the beta -2-glycoprotein i (a serum protein) in serum and other body fluids. measurement of beta -2-glycoprotein i aids in the diagnosis of an inherited deficiency of this serum protein. 
(b) classification. class i (general controls). the device is exempt from the premarket notification procedures in subpart e of part 807 of this chapter subject to  866.9.
