filings; address; hours. 
documents required or permitted to be filed in, and correspondence relating to, hearings governed by the regulations in this chapter shall be filed with the hearing clerk, drug enforcement administration. see the table of dea mailing addresses in  1321.01 of this chapter for the current mailing address. this office is open monday through friday from 8:30 a.m. to 5 p.m. eastern standard or daylight saving time, whichever is effective in the district of columbia at the time, except on national legal holidays. documents shall be dated and deemed filed upon receipt by the hearing clerk.
